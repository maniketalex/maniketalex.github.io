About Department

Three year vocational course in computer Application was started in 2003,by the order of the then vice-chancellor,T.M Bhagalpur University Bhagalpur vide letter no B/18634 dated 19/21 May 2003 under self finance scheme .The deparment has state of the art infrastructure like ac computer labs with broadband connectivity,smart classroom updated library with interner facilities and photocopier machine spacious lecture hall and recreation and amenity centre are added attraction.

Students who have passed out from here achieved their goal of life and found placement in software companies banks insurance companies and computer training institute etc. The department is being run by an efficient managment committee with principal as the chairman .Other members include the Bursur and the Coordinate of all vocational courses of the college like Biotechnology and drugand pharmaceutical Chemistry etc.

About College

T.N.B. College is the second oldest institution of higher learning in Bihar, second to Patna College of Patna only. The College aims to inculcate among the students the fine human values for enlightenment of their personalities so that they become agents of positive change in the contemporary times and make contribute meaningfully to the society and the nation at large..
